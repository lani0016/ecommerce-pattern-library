Free as a bird cancer care kits

Cancer sucks.Your care shouldn't.

Free as a bird is a care kit service that allows patients to feel human again. We hand pick and then deliver kits that allow our customers to focus on healing while letting them be as comfortable as possible.Our goal is to make this disease easier to recover from so 75% of our proceeds go directly toward helping those who may not be able to afford our kits; As well as going back into the company so that our prices can stay at the lowest price for cost available.the other 25% of proceeds goes towards cancer reseach donations in order to hopefully give our customers a brighter future to look forward too!
